Meet Sabah

My journey: From the Ring to Building Foundations

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) was more than a sport for me. For years, I trained hard to master every punch, every kick, every take-down and every submission. The discipline, focus, and heart I developed in the cage shaped who I am today.

Over time, I realized that I wasn't just fighting inside the cage, but rather problem solving. Every opponent, like every offer and negotiation in Real Estate, presents it's own challenges that need solving. Unlike Real Estate, problems inside of the cage need to be solved in matter of seconds and that requires decisions to be made on the spot. Real Estate gives us the gift of time to be able to make those hard decisions. I will be there in your corner helping and guiding you every step of the way. With all of the relative information at your disposal you can make the best decision. Ultimately it is the right choices and decisions that lead to success and achieving your goals.

That is why I stepped into real estate. I want to guide people confidently through their journeys without the confusion and overwhelm I once saw too often. I bring the mindset of a fighter and the precision of a strategist to every client. Your wins are personal to me, and I am here to help you claim them with preparation, focus, and heart.

What keeps me going

  • Resilience built from years of training under pressure
  • Deep connection to the communities I serve
  • Unwavering support because no one should face this journey alone
  • My family (My wife, two kids and my parents)

Every story is unique. Every move is personal.

Whether you are buying your first home or growing your investments, my focus remains the same: honest advice, sharp strategy, and full commitment. Your future deserves more than a deal. It deserves intention.
